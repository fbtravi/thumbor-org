open-source smart on-demand image cropping, resizing and filters
LEARN MORE

Thumbor is a smart imaging service. It enables on-demand crop, resizing and flipping of images. It features a very smart detection of important points in the image for better cropping and resizing, using state-of-the-art face and feature detection algorithms (more on that in Detection Algorithms).

Save time and money in your company with Thumbor.

GET STARTED or see the documentation

No more headless bodies

With the most advanced face detection technology, Thumbor ensure you to have the best image crop possible. Everytime a face is detected, it will crop the image around it, making sure the most important parts of the picture are preserved.

Images everywhere

Thumbor allows users to store and load images from anywhere needed. It's really simple to implement a new loader or storage. It comes packaged with file, http, redis and mongo implementations for loaders and storages. Just pick what makes sense for you or implement your own.

Filter to enhance

Thumbor has support for image filters. Filters usually apply some post-processing to the images and they can be chained and applied in order. Thumbor's extensible nature allows you to write your own filters, but it comes with some pre-packaged filters of its own. Brightness, contrast, grayscale, red-eye and blur are a few samples.

Thumbor supports webp

WebP is a new format for lossy and lossless images that compresses a lot better than jpeg and png. Thumbor allows users to specify that browsers that support WebP automatically get it. This can have a dramatic increase in the performance of your website. Besides WebP, Thumbor also uses some tactics to reduce the file size even further.

BENEFITS

what’s in it for you

FOR DEVELOPERS
Remember that time when you had to re-generate all your images due to a new size? Thumbor makes all that pain go away. With a comprehensive set of libraries that support it in several languages, as well as loads of plug-ins, you'll never need another imaging server.
FOR OPERATIONS
Thumbor decouples imaging from storage and loading. Unparalleled extensibility allows easy tailoring to your own infrastructure requirements. Store your images the way you want. Off-load OpenCV processing to a specific farm? Thumbor's your best friend. Use NFS to store images? It's done!
FOR DESIGNERS
Want to change the size of all images on your site without suffering? Just use a new size and Thumbor will do the hard work. High quality pictures without compromising performance? Check. Best possible User Experience? Check. Thumbor's got your back.
FOR PROJECT MANAGERS
Save time and money by removing imaging stories from your backlog. Thumbor's a complete solution, ranging from uploading images to rendering them with smart face-recognition cropping. Need a rotated, black & white, rounded-border version of your images? You got it!
